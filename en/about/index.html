About

I speak code, AI, and business.

Started as a frontend developer, now founder, AI engineer, and Fractional CAIO. This mix — code, product, business — is my leverage: I understand what tech teams need, and what leadership wants to see.

Get in touch → My services

From developer to founder

My career started where tech and business intersect — writing code for one of the world's largest automotive companies.

While studying at Hochschule Pforzheim (B.Sc. Digital Enterprise Management), I worked as an IT Consultant at Mercedes-Benz Tech Motion and later as a Frontend Developer and DevOps Engineer at S-IT (Daimler Trucks context). I learned there how enterprise software really works: with legacy, compliance, 10-year roadmaps, and teams larger than entire startups.

In 2023 I built imagetocaption.ai alongside my job — a SaaS tool that automatically generates social media captions. Solo. No funding. Within 12 months, 350,000 people used it.

In 2024 I joined generic.de as AI Engineer, building enterprise RAG systems, GitHub Copilot rollouts, and AI lead scoring. In 2025 I formed TAISC – The AI Software Company GmbH out of imagetocaption, headquartered in Pforzheim/Karlsruhe. Today I combine both: deep understanding of how enterprise IT ticks — and the speed of a solo founder shipping software.
