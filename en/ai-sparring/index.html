When AI Sparring makes sense

  • You're a tech lead or founder who regularly faces AI decisions — but your team is too deep in day-to-day work for an outside perspective
  • You're not planning a 12-month implementation, but need a sparring session every 1–2 weeks
  • You want a second opinion on your architecture, tool, or vendor choice
  • You have an important tech decision you want to present to someone outside your team who will give you an honest answer

What's included

  • One- or multi-hour sparring calls, scheduled on your timeline
  • An honest second opinion on architecture, tool, and vendor choices
  • Code and architecture reviews within the agreed scope
  • Prepared recommendations on specific questions (no 50-page doc — bullet points with rationale)
  • No implementation component. If you need code, that's the Forward Deployed Engineer.
