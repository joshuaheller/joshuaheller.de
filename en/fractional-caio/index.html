What exactly is a Fractional CAIO?

A Fractional CAIO (Chief AI Officer) is an experienced AI and tech leader who works for multiple companies simultaneously — each part-time, but with real strategic ownership.

Unlike an Interim CAIO who bridges a vacancy: I\'m not a transitional solution. I\'m your long-term solution. There\'s no end date and no search for a successor.

The advantage: you get senior expertise that would cost €150,000–300,000 per year as a full-time position — at a fraction of that cost.

Flexible commitment.

1–2 days per week, depending on need. Remote-first with regular on-site days.

Open-ended engagement.

No project with an end date. As long as it makes sense.

Monthly retainer.

Predictable costs instead of day-rate surprises. From €3,000/month net.

Fractional CAIO vs. Interim CAIO vs. Consultant

Fractional CAIO

Permanent external AI leadership part-time (1–2 days/week). Strategic focus. Open-ended. Monthly retainer.

Interim CAIO

Bridges a crisis or vacancy. Often full-time, time-limited, operational focus, high day rate. Not what I do.

Tech Consultant

Analyzes, evaluates, recommends — without ongoing ownership. For one-off questions: see AI Sparring.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a Fractional CAIO cost?

I work as Fractional CAIO exclusively on retainer, from €3,000 net per month — the exact amount depends on the agreed hour commitment. Compared to a full-time position (€150–300k annual salary plus overhead), it's a predictable, significantly more affordable investment.

How many hours per week?

Typically 8–16 hours. We define the commitment together and adjust it quarterly.

How does communication work?

Via the tools you already use — e.g., Microsoft Teams, Notion, GitHub. Weekly call with CEO or tech lead, monthly written report, quarterly strategy review.

What sets you apart from a consulting firm?

A consulting firm delivers recommendations. I take ownership. I decide alongside you (or alone, depending on authority granted), and I'm still there in 12 months when the consequences of my decisions become visible.

What's the difference between FDE and Fractional CAIO?

FDE = I write code in your codebase. Fractional CAIO = I make and communicate AI decisions with/for your leadership. Many engagements combine both.

What industries and company sizes do you work with?

From mid-market companies and scaleups to large enterprises and private equity portfolios — across industrial, B2B SaaS, and software. Working with multiple clients in the same industry is an advantage: I bring patterns from comparable setups.
