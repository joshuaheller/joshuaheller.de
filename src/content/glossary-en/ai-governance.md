## What does this mean?

AI governance encompasses policies, processes, and controls that ensure AI is used responsibly, transparently, and in compliance with applicable laws.

## How it works

Organizations define policies for AI usage, assess risks, document decisions, and maintain audit trails. This includes ethical guardrails and compliance checks.

## Example

A company creates an AI policy that specifies which data may be used, who must approve AI systems, and how outputs are documented.

## Why it matters

With the EU AI Act and growing AI adoption, governance is becoming mandatory. Organizations need clear rules before deploying AI in production.
