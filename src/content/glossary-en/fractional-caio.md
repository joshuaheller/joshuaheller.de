## What is a Fractional CAIO?

A Fractional CAIO (Chief AI Officer) takes on AI and technology leadership part-time, typically one to two days per week. Unlike an Interim CAIO who bridges a vacancy or crisis, a Fractional CAIO is not a transitional solution but a permanent arrangement without an end date.

The model originated in the US and UK, where Fractional C-suite executives have long been standard for startups, mid-market companies, and enterprises. The advantage: senior expertise that would cost €150,000–300,000 per year as a full-time role — at a fraction of that cost.

## Example Use Case

A mid-market company or enterprise knows AI is strategically important but has no one responsible for it. A full-time CAIO isn't financially justified. A Fractional CAIO develops the AI roadmap, makes tech stack decisions, leads the team, and reports to the executive board.
