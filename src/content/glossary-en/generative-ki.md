## What does this mean?

Generative AI refers to systems that can produce new content rather than just analyzing existing data. This includes models like ChatGPT, DALL-E, and Claude.

## How it works

GenAI models learn the structure and patterns from training data and can then generate similar but new content — word by word, pixel by pixel.

## Example

Automated text generation for marketing, code generation, document summarization, or image creation.

## Why it matters

GenAI has made AI accessible to millions of users and enables entirely new kinds of automation.
