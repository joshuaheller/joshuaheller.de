## What does this mean?

Human-in-the-loop means that a person is involved at important or uncertain decision points. The AI works autonomously, but at predefined points human approval is required.

## How it works

The AI agent handles routine tasks independently. For cases that exceed predefined thresholds (e.g., cost > €500 or confidence < 80%), the system escalates to a human.

## Example

A customer service agent automatically answers standard questions but routes complaints and edge cases to a human agent.

## Why it matters

Human-in-the-loop is the key to trustworthy AI in enterprise deployments — automation with a safety net.
