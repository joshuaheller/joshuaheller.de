## What does this mean?

Artificial intelligence describes systems that automate human-like tasks such as understanding, decision-making, and communication. Today AI primarily encompasses machine learning and generative models.

## How it works

AI systems learn from data, recognize patterns, and make decisions based on them — without being explicitly programmed for each situation.

## Example

A chatbot that understands customer inquiries and responds independently, or a system that automatically processes invoices.

## Why it matters

AI is the foundational technology behind all modern automation — from chatbots to autonomous agents.
