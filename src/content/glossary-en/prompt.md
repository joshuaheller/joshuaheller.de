## What does this mean?

A prompt is the text you send to an AI model — a question, instruction, or context. The quality of the prompt largely determines the quality of the response.

## How it works

The model reads your prompt and generates the most likely continuation. The more precise and structured your prompt, the better the result.

## Example

"Summarize this contract in 3 bullet points" is a concrete prompt. "Tell me something about the contract" is a vague prompt that produces worse results.

## Why it matters

Prompt design is a core skill in working with AI — the right instruction makes the difference between useless and brilliant output.
